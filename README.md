## 🧩 All-in-one platforms

> Why buy five tools when you can be overwhelmed by one?

Platforms that combine multiple research and analytics capabilities — session replay, analytics, feature flags, surveys, and more — in a single product.

- [Contentsquare](https://contentsquare.com) - Digital experience analytics combining heatmaps, session replay, and journey analysis.
- [Datadog](https://datadoghq.com) - Observability platform with RUM, session replay, and user journey analytics.
- [Hotjar](https://hotjar.com) - Heatmaps, session replay, surveys, and feedback widgets in one platform.
- [Pendo](https://pendo.io) - Product analytics, in-app guides, session replay, and feedback in one platform.
- [PostHog](https://posthog.com) - Open source suite: analytics, session replay, feature flags, surveys, and A/B testing.
- [Screeb](https://screeb.app) - Product analytics, session replay, and in-product surveys in one platform.

## 💬 Survey

### 📋 In-app surveys

> "On a scale of 1 to 5, how likely are you to abandon this survey before finishing it?"

Ask questions along your users' journey.

- [Hotjar](https://hotjar.com) - On-site polls and feedback widgets alongside heatmaps.
- [Pendo](https://pendo.io) - In-app surveys and NPS tied directly to product usage data.
- [PostHog](https://posthog.com) - In-product surveys integrated with analytics and feature flags.
- [Refiner](https://refiner.io) - In-product microsurveys for SaaS.
- [Screeb](https://screeb.app) - User feedback and in-product surveys.
- [Sprig](https://sprig.com) - In-product surveys and concept testing tied to real user segments.

### 🔬 Feedback analysis

> We have 10.000 pieces of user feedback. They all say "make it faster." Faster than what?

Aggregate, tag, and extract signal from qualitative feedback at scale.

- [Chattermill](https://chattermill.com) - AI-powered feedback analytics for CX and product teams.
- [Enterpret](https://enterpret.com) - AI-native feedback analysis aggregating support tickets, reviews, and NPS at scale.
- [Kapiche](https://kapiche.com) - AI-driven theme discovery and clustering from unstructured customer feedback.
- [Lumoa](https://lumoa.me) - AI feedback analytics for sentiment and themes across NPS, surveys, and support data.
- [Thematic](https://getthematic.com) - AI-powered thematic analysis for clustering recurring themes from open-ended feedback.

## 🔍 Product discovery

> We built the feature users asked for. Turns out they meant something else entirely.

Build an understanding of customers, then using that knowledge to prioritize features development.

- [Canny](https://canny.io) - Capture, organize, and analyze product feedback.
- [Harvestr](https://harvestr.io) - Customer feedback and product management software.
- [Productboard](https://www.productboard.com) - Product management software centered on customer insights.

## 🎥 Session recording

> Big Browser is watching you click the wrong button again.

Session recordings are renderings of real actions taken by visitors as they browse a website. Recordings capture mouse movement, clicks, taps, and scrolling across multiple pages on desktop and mobile devices.

- [Contentsquare](https://contentsquare.com) - Session replay with zone-based heatmaps and journey analysis.
- [Datadog](https://datadoghq.com) - RUM and session replay tied to backend traces and errors.
- [Fullstory](https://fullstory.com) - Behavioral data platform with session replay and real-time error capture.
- [Heap](https://heap.io) - Session replay linked to auto-captured user behavior data.
- [Hotjar](https://hotjar.com) - Website heatmaps and behavior analytics tools.
- [Microsoft Clarity](https://clarity.microsoft.com) - Free heatmaps and session recordings.
- [PostHog](https://posthog.com) - Open source session replay integrated with product analytics.
- [Screeb](https://screeb.app) - Session replay with mobile support and product analytics integration.
- [Smartlook](https://smartlook.com) - Qualitative analytics with session recordings and heatmaps.

## 📊 Analytics

### 📈 Product analytics

> Our DAU is up 200%! (We changed how we define "active".)

Purpose-built tools for understanding user behavior, retention, and product usage.

- [Amplitude](https://amplitude.com) - Digital analytics platform for product and marketing teams.
- [Datadog](https://datadoghq.com) - Observability platform with RUM, session replay, and user journey analytics.
- [Heap](https://heap.io) - Digital insights platform that auto-captures all user interactions.
- [Mixpanel](https://mixpanel.com) - Self-serve product analytics for user behavior.
- [PostHog](https://posthog.com) - Open source product analytics, feature flags, and A/B testing.
- [Screeb](https://screeb.app) - Product analytics with built-in user feedback and session replay.

### 📣 Marketing analytics

> We got 10,000 visitors! Only 3 converted, but the traffic slide looked great in the deck.

Tools focused on acquisition, conversion, and campaign performance across web and digital channels.

- [Contentsquare](https://contentsquare.com) - Digital experience analytics with journey mapping, zone analysis, and conversion insights.
- [Google Analytics](https://analytics.google.com) - Web and app analytics by Google.

### 🏛️ Business intelligence

> A SQL query walks into a bar, walks up to two tables and asks... "Can I join you?"

General-purpose querying and visualization tools for slicing data across sources.

- [Looker](https://looker.com) - BI and data analytics platform by Google Cloud.
- [Metabase](https://metabase.com) - Open source business intelligence and analytics.
- [Redash](https://redash.io) - Open source tool for querying and visualizing data.

### 🗄️ Customer data platform

> Your data is in 12 places. None of them agree on the user's name.

A customer data platform is a collection of software which creates a persistent, unified customer database that is accessible to other systems. Data is pulled from multiple sources, cleaned and combined to create a single customer profile. This structured data is then made available to other marketing systems.

- [mParticle](https://mparticle.com) - Customer data platform for mobile and web.
- [RudderStack](https://rudderstack.com) - Open source platform for streaming data from your website to any modern stack.
- [Segment](https://segment.com) - Collect, clean, and activate your customer data.
- [Tealium](https://tealium.com) - Real-time customer data hub and tag management.

## 🚩 Feature flags & experimentation

> We ran an A/B test. Variant B won. No one remembered to turn off variant A.

Control feature rollouts and run rigorous experiments to validate product decisions with data.

- [Flagsmith](https://flagsmith.com) - Open source feature flags and remote config.
- [GrowthBook](https://growthbook.io) - Open source feature flags and A/B testing platform.
- [LaunchDarkly](https://launchdarkly.com) - Feature management and experimentation platform.
- [Optimizely](https://optimizely.com) - A/B testing and feature experimentation at enterprise scale.
- [PostHog](https://posthog.com) - Open source feature flags and A/B testing integrated with analytics.
- [Split.io](https://split.io) - Feature flags with built-in impact measurement.
- [VWO](https://vwo.com) - A/B testing and conversion optimization platform.

## 🗺️ Product tours & onboarding

> Our onboarding is so good, users still get lost — they just feel great about it.

Guide users through your product, drive adoption, and collect in-context feedback.

- [Appcues](https://appcues.com) - No-code user onboarding and product tours.
- [Intercom](https://intercom.com) - In-app messaging, product tours, and customer support in one platform.
- [Pendo](https://pendo.io) - In-app guides and walkthroughs tied to product analytics.
- [Screeb](https://screeb.app) - In-app product tours and onboarding flows tied to user segments and analytics.

## 🧪 User testing

> It takes 5 users to find 85% of usability issues. It takes 1 stakeholder to ignore all of them.

Validate designs and flows with real users before shipping.

- [Maze](https://maze.co) - Rapid product research platform for usability testing.
- [Sprig](https://sprig.com) - In-product concept and prototype testing tied to real user segments.
- [UserTesting](https://usertesting.com) - On-demand human insight platform.

## 🎙️ User interview

> "Tell me about the last time you used our product." — "What product?"

Conduct, record, and analyze user interviews at scale.

- [Discuss](https://discuss.io) - Qual research platform for live and async moderated interviews.
- [Grain](https://grain.com) - AI-powered meeting recorder and highlight reel for user interviews.
- [Great Question](https://greatquestion.co) - End-to-end interview platform with scheduling, recording, and transcription.
- [Lookback](https://lookback.com) - Live and async moderated interview sessions with real-time team observation.
- [Lyssna](https://lyssna.com) - Remote research platform for moderated and unmoderated user interviews.
- [Outset](https://outset.ai) - AI-moderated interviews that scale qualitative research without a human moderator.

## 🗂️ Research repository

> Where insights go to be forgotten by everyone except the person who ran the study.

Centralize, organize, and make discoverable all research artifacts — transcripts, videos, highlights, and insights — across studies and teams.

- [Condens](https://condens.io) - Lightweight research repository for centralizing and tagging research data.
- [Dovetail](https://dovetailapp.com) - Research repository with AI-powered tagging, theming, and insight synthesis.
- [Marvin](https://heymarvin.com) - AI research assistant for tagging, analysis, and insight generation across studies.

## 🎯 User recruitment

> Finding the right participant is harder than finding a bug in production on a Friday.

Find and recruit the right participants for your research.

- [Prolific](https://prolific.com) - High-quality participant recruitment for research studies.
- [Respondent](https://respondent.io) - B2B and consumer research participant recruitment.
- [User Interviews](https://userinterviews.com) - The fastest way to recruit research participants.

### 📖 Books

Essential reading for product managers and UX researchers.

- [Continuous Discovery Habits](https://www.producttalk.org/2021/05/continuous-discovery-habits) - Teresa Torres. How to build a habit of weekly customer touchpoints to make better product decisions.
- [Demand-Side Sales 101](https://www.demandsidesales.com) - Bob Moesta. Selling and building products through the lens of Jobs to be Done.
- [Interviewing Users](https://portigal.com/Books/interviewing-users) - Steve Portigal. How to uncover compelling insights through conversations with users.
- [Just Enough Research](https://abookapart.com/products/just-enough-research) - Erika Hall. A practical guide to doing research that is useful, timely, and cost-effective.
- [Lean UX](https://www.oreilly.com/library/view/lean-ux-3rd/9781098116507) - Jeff Gothelf & Josh Seiden. Applying lean principles to UX design and research.
- [Research That Scales](https://www.researchops.community/book) - Kate Towsey. Building and managing a research practice at scale.
- [The Mom Test](https://www.momtestbook.com) - Rob Fitzpatrick. How to talk to customers and learn if your business idea is good.
- [The User Experience Team of One](https://rosenfeldmedia.com/books/the-user-experience-team-of-one) - Leah Buley. A research and design survival guide for solo practitioners.
